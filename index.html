How To Use

Your step-by-step guide to trading like a pro. It's easier than it looks, promise!

1

Set Up Your Profile 🐱

Click the 👤 icon in the top right. Add your display name, buddy, title, and any social links so other traders can find you. Your profile code lets people look you up quickly.

2

Add Items to Your Inventory 🎒

Head to Inventory and add the bags you own. Mark any duplicates — those are your trading currency! The platform tracks quantity and availability automatically.

3

Build Your Wishlist 💫

Go to Wishlist and add items you're hunting for. Set a priority (1 = must have!) and desired quantity. The platform will help match you with traders who have them listed.

4

Create a Listing 📋

Click + List to put an item up for trade. You can specify what you want in return, or leave it open. Choose your trade mode: direct swap, open offer, or collection match.

5

Make or Receive Offers 🤝

Browse Listings and make offers by selecting items from your inventory. The Fairness Meter tells you if your offer is underpaying, fair, or generous — based on wiki values.

6

Complete the Trade ✅

Once both traders confirm, the trade is marked complete and your reputation score updates. If something goes wrong, you can open a dispute and an admin will review.

💡

Pro tip: Click any item thumbnail anywhere on the site to see its full details — tier, rarity, demand, wiki value, and a link to the wiki page.
